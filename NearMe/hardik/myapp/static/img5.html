Alangulam

Tamil Nadu Cements Corporation Limited operates the famous Alangulam Cement Works, which is one of the oldest government cement factories in India. The factory is located in Alangulam in Virudhunagar district and started its production in 1970. It was the first cement plant in India established under the public sector. The factory mainly manufactures ARASU brand cement, including Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC) and Portland Pozzolana Cement (PPC). The cement produced here is widely used for construction works such as bridges, dams, buildings, and government projects. The factory provides employment opportunities for many people directly and indirectly, helping the economic development of the surrounding areas. Modernization works have also been carried out to improve production and technology in the plant.
