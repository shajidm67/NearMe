Pavoorchatram

Vennaimalai Murugan Temple is a famous Murugan temple located near Pavoorchatram in Tenkasi district. The temple is situated on a small hill surrounded by beautiful natural scenery and greenery, which attracts many devotees and tourists. Lord Murugan is worshipped here as the main deity, and devotees visit the temple to seek blessings for peace, courage, and success in life. The temple is known for its calm atmosphere and spiritual importance. Many people climb the hill steps to reach the temple and enjoy the scenic view from the top. Festivals such as Thaipusam and Panguni Uthiram are celebrated grandly, drawing large numbers of devotees every year. The temple is also an important religious and tourist attraction in the Tenkasi region.
