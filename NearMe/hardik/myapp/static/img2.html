Courtallam

Courtallam is a famous tourist place in Tenkasi district. It is well known for its waterfalls and natural beauty. Thousands of tourists visit Courtallam every year. The waterfalls are believed to have medicinal properties. Main Falls, Five Falls, and Old Falls are popular attractions. The climate in Courtallam is cool and pleasant. Many people enjoy bathing in the waterfalls. The area is surrounded by green hills and forests. Courtallam is also called the Spa of South India. It is one of the best tourist destinations in Tamil Nadu.
