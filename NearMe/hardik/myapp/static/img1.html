Thoranamalai Murugan Kovil

Thoranamalai Murugan Temple is a famous hill temple in Tenkasi district. The temple is dedicated to Lord Murugan. It is located on a beautiful hill surrounded by nature. Devotees climb many steps to reach the temple. The temple is peaceful and attracts many tourists. Festivals are celebrated grandly during special occasions. The hill provides a beautiful view of the surrounding area. Many devotees visit the temple to seek blessings. The temple is known for its spiritual importance. Thoranamalai is one of the important tourist places near Tenkasi.
